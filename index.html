About Me

I’m Kajal Pandey — a graphic designer with 3 years of professional experience in crafting thoughtful, visually compelling brand identities, print designs, and digital creatives. My approach to design is rooted in simplicity, purpose, and storytelling — ensuring every visual leaves a lasting impression. While my core expertise lies in graphic design, I also have a strong foundational knowledge in video editing, web design, and UI/UX. This allows me to understand the broader creative landscape and deliver designs that work seamlessly across mediums. I'm constantly exploring, learning, and evolving — driven by curiosity and a passion for clean, impactful design.
